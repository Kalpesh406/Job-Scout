About us

Company Information

Job Scout is a website which is for students and companies, it helps students to apply for different jobs and internships of their liking based on their qualification and for companies to host exams for recruiting prospects. Different features such as CV builder, Forums, Startup helping, Teammate finding, and archives are also included.

Goals

Helping students to find out what they like doing best and they get someone to pay them for doing it.

Achievement

Successfully created website and working on it to increase engagements and helpful features.
